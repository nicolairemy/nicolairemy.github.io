Nicolai Remy creates custom timepieces that are perfect to give out at any occasion: onboarding new employees, welcoming new investors, celebrating the success of a project - all deserve the pomp and circumstance of a stylish watch.

Corporate gifts usually consist of water bottles, hats, and t-shirts. We're engineers, we know. We love these gifts and wear them all the time when we're lounging around on weekends, but how about a gift that complements a tailored suit as well as summer shorts?

These are beautiful timepieces that can be customized to portray your company's image: we don't just throw a logo onto the watchface and ship it, we will work with your designers to find the perfect way to emphasize your corporate ideals with the bracelet style, the metal color, even the box the watch is delivered in. We have the production capacity to meet any quantity requirements. Check out some of our past designs below!
